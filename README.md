# NutriLink Hub

A human-centered AI platform helping NYC seniors discover food assistance, healthcare referrals, and community resources.

## Problem

NYC seniors face fragmented food systems, digital barriers, and disconnected services.

## Solution

NutriLink Hub combines:

- AI-assisted eligibility matching
- Human navigation
- Community hubs
- Healthcare referrals
- Culturally relevant food recommendations

## Highlights

- 20+ stakeholder interviews
- 7+ product iterations
- Human-centered design
- Product strategy
- Service design
